How Forming Paws works

Four steps from profile to a safe, well-documented match.

1

Create a profile

Add your dog's breed, age, temperament, and photos. Owners verify their identity; dogs get their own profile page.

2

Upload health records

Vet wellness exams, vaccinations, and breed-specific screenings go into a private health vault. Our team reviews and verifies them.

3

Match nearby

Filter by breed, sex, age, and distance. Express interest — when it's mutual, chat unlocks so owners can talk first.

4

Meet safely

We suggest neutral meeting locations and a record-exchange checklist, so both owners meet prepared and confident.

Health first — it's the whole point

Forming Paws exists to raise the standard of dog breeding, not just to make introductions.

🩺

Verified health vault

Matching stays locked until baseline vet documentation is reviewed. A green verified badge means real, checked records — not an honor system.

❤️‍🩹

A path to healthy

Dogs whose records don't pass aren't rejected — they're referred to partner veterinarians with a plan to bring their health up to standard.

🚫

Built against puppy mills

Litter caps per profile, mandatory documentation, and community reporting keep high-volume breeders off the platform.
